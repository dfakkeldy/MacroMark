// the flow

From thought to vault in four beats.

No "Done" button. No silence timeout. The watch does the timestamp; your phone does the filing.

01

Speak

Tap the capture/instant complication. SFSpeechRecognizer starts transcribing immediately and never times out from silence.

02

Wrist-down save

Lower your wrist and scenePhase changes — your text and the precise origin timestamp are saved at once.

03

Sync

WatchConnectivity queues the payload and delivers it to your iPhone, even in the background.

04

Append

The Macro Engine formats your words and appends them to YYYY-MM-DD.md in iCloud Drive.

// why it's different

Built for people who live in plain text.

*

Zero-friction capture

Tap, speak, drop your wrist. The note lands instantly with an accurate, watch-generated timestamp — no buttons to fumble.

*

The append mechanic

Every capture for a day joins one YYYY-MM-DD.md daily note, instead of scattering hundreds of tiny files across your vault.

*

Verbal macros

Say a trigger word to format text, or drop in {date}, {time}, and {location} variables to build real workflows.

*

Liquid Glass watch UI

Large microphone and keyboard buttons for instant input, plus one-tap access to today's daily log — right from your wrist.

*

PKM-native

Writes plain-text Markdown to a dedicated iCloud Documents folder — instantly readable by Obsidian, Logseq, and friends.

*

On-device & yours

Dictation runs through Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer. Your words go to your vault — not a server.

// verbal macros

Say it. Watch it format.

Trigger words become Markdown. Variables expand on the fly. Make your own in Settings → Macros.

you say "Heading One"→# heading
you say "Quote"→> blockquote
you type {date}→2026-06-20
you type {time}→15:27
you type {location}→your current place

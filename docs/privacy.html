Privacy Policy

MacroMark is built for local capture into your own Markdown files. The app does not sell personal data, does not include third-party analytics in v1.0, and does not require an account.

MacroMark uses microphone and speech recognition access when you dictate notes, location access only when you use a location macro, iCloud Drive or a selected folder to save Markdown daily notes, and StoreKit to manage purchases.

Captured notes, audio waiting for delivery, macro settings, and export status stay on your devices or in your iCloud Drive. Contact support at dan@kinnokilabs.com.
